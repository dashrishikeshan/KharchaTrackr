Privacy Policy for KharchaTrackr

Last updated: May 10, 2026

KharchaTrackr is an Android expense tracking app developed by Logic IT Services. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

KharchaTrackr may collect and process the following information:

  • Google account information used for sign-in, such as name, email address, profile photo, and user ID.
  • Profile information entered by the user, such as name, date of birth, profession, and profile photo/avatar.
  • Expense, income, Udhari, loan, category, amount, date, note, and sharing data entered by the user.
  • Shared ledger access details, such as participant email addresses and view/edit roles.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data processed by Google AdMob.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, including Google sign-in, cloud sync, shared expense books, profile management, expense tracking, and advertising. Data is also used to maintain app security, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability.

Third-Party Services

KharchaTrackr uses third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication for Google sign-in.
  • Cloud Firestore for storing and syncing app data.
  • Google AdMob for displaying ads.
  • Google Sign-In services.

You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Permissions

KharchaTrackr may request permissions such as camera or photo access for profile images and internet access for login, sync, and ads. Permissions are used only to support app features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. User-entered ledger data may be shared with other users only when the user grants access to a shared ledger by email. Google services may process data as necessary to provide authentication, database, analytics, and advertising functionality.

Data Retention and Deletion

App data is retained while the user continues using KharchaTrackr. Users may delete entries inside the app where deletion is available. For account or data deletion requests, contact us using the email below.

Children's Privacy

KharchaTrackr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use Firebase and Google services to help protect account access and cloud data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
