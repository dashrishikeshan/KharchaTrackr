KharchaTrackr Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: May 10, 2026

KharchaTrackr users can request deletion of their account data and app data by contacting Logic IT Services.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to contact@KharchaTrackr.online from the same Google account email address used to sign in to KharchaTrackr.

Use the subject line:

KharchaTrackr Account Deletion Request

Data Deleted

After verifying the request, we will delete or anonymize app data associated with your account where technically possible, including:

  • User profile information stored for KharchaTrackr.
  • Expense, income, Udhari, loan, note, and category records created by your account.
  • Shared ledger access data associated with your email address.

Data That May Be Retained

Some records may be retained for a limited period if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, backup, or compliance reasons. Shared ledger records created by other users may remain visible to those users unless deletion is requested by the ledger owner or required by law.

Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days after verifying the request.
