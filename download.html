Setup guide

Make KharchaTrackr useful from the first day.

Choose your main categories

Start with 8 to 12 simple categories: rent, food, travel, bills, health, shopping, education, business, subscriptions, and savings.

Add recurring payments

Enter rent, EMIs, electricity, internet, insurance, school fees, and subscriptions so fixed costs are visible early.

Create realistic budgets

Use your current habits as the baseline. A good first budget is realistic enough to follow and clear enough to improve.

Review every weekend

A short weekly review helps you catch overspending before the month ends. Small adjustments are easier than last-day panic.
